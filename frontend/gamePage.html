    Game Rules

    🎯 Objective

    Grow your cell by eating food and smaller players while avoiding being eaten by larger ones.

    🎮 Controls

    Desktop:

    • Mouse: Move your cell around the map
    • Space: Split your cell to move faster or attack
    • W: Eject mass to feed other players or lose weight
    • Shift: Use speed boost (consumes speed points)

    Mobile:

    • Joystick: Move your cell around the map
    • Split Button: Split your cell to move faster or attack
    • Feed Button: Eject mass to feed other players or lose weight
    • Speed up Button: Use speed boost (consumes speed points)

    📈 Gameplay

    • Eat colored dots to grow your cell
    • Larger cells can eat smaller cells
    • Split cells will automatically merge back together over time
    • Speed decreases as you grow larger
    • Use speed boost strategically to escape or catch prey
    • Feeding ejects 10% mass only from your biggest cell

    💡 Tips

    • Stay near the edges when small to avoid larger players
    • Split to escape from larger cells
    • Team up with other players by feeding them
    • Watch the leaderboard to track top players

    ⚠️ Important Notes

    • If keyboard controls don't work, check your language settings - use English keyboard layout
    • Key "W" for feeding may not work with non-English keyboards (e.g., Ukrainian)
    • In some game modes, feeding acts as projectiles that can damage bots
